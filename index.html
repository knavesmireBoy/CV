PROFILE

Self-motivated and largely self-taught individual who made a decisive contribution to the success and development of the - part - family printing business. Over 35 years I had to master a range of disciplines joining an industry soon to pivot towards an entirely digital workflow.

Attention to detail was critical in all my roles and front of mind when mentoring new recruits. In more recent years the related virtues of focus and tenacity have served me well in negotiating the increasingly complex field of web development, things having moved on somewhat since I completed this web design course. I've progressed too, finally convincing myself that I have enough grounding in the key skills and more than enough enthusiasm to learn new ones to make a positive contribution to this corner of the economy.
